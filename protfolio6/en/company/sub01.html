Company Introduction

Powerful Synergy - YURA
Comprised of 3 affiliated companies; Yura Corporation, Yura Harness and Yura Tech, Yura is striving to become a recognized name in the global automotive parts industry. Yura Corporation and Yura Harness supply automobile wiring harness. Yura Tech manufacturers a variety of ignition system products such as spark plug and stick coil. Yura’s affiliated companies are working closely to create powerful synergy, which is the intangible strength that differentiate Yura from other competitors.
