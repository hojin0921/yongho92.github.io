CEO message

ecoming the global leader in the development of future automobile components The modern automobile is no longer just a simple means of transportation to move from one place to another. It has broadened its traditional role by taking on a new role of being a combined living space on the road. The rapid advancement of modern technology has enabled us to conduct business or even shop in our cars. Today’s automobile has been continuously evolving to become the aggregate of cutting-edge technology that offers safety, convenience, and comfort. Starting as an automobile ignition system parts manufacturer in 1987, Yura has been aggressively and successfully expanding her business capacity within the electric and electronic automotive parts industry by diversifying her product portfolio that includes the wiring harness, BEC, and more. In particular, Yura’s key product, the wiring harness, performs a quintessential role in bridging a multitude of complex state-of-art electronic systems in the automobile to work in unison. From our jumble beginning, Yura has become a reputable name in global automotive parts industry with 7 manufacturing plants, 3 Research and Development (R&D) Centers, and 4 distributions centers in the republic of Korea. Yura also has 11 manufacturing outposts in 6 Different countries including China, Slovakia, the Czech Republic, Tunisia, Vietnam, and Russia.

Yura will continuously and actively give impetus to quality innovation and advanced technology development. Furthermore, we will be the vanguard of leading the latest technological trends of automobiles such as increase of high-tech electronic equipment, reduction of weight, and greater modulation of automotive parts. As the prospect for commercialization of hybrid and fuel cell powered automobile is expected to increase due to the strengthening of environmental control, high price of energy, and exhaustion of fossil fuel, our effort to become one of the most recognizable faces in eco-friendly automotive parts will be also strengthened.\our diligent practice of “Quality First” management policy for the past 20years has made the Yura of today. Now, Yura is looking forward to becoming a global leader in the automotive parts industry of the 21st century by endlessly challenging ourselves and continuously investing in Research and Development.

Yura Corporation CEO
Ohm Dae Yeol
